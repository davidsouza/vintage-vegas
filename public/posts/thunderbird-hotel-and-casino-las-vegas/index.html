Remembering the Thunderbird Hotel and Casino: A Vintage Las Vegas Strip Icon

A Southwestern Oasis on the Strip

The Las Vegas Strip wasn’t always the glittering behemoth it is today. Back in 1948, it was a burgeoning stretch of Highway 91, and the Thunderbird Hotel and Casino was a vital piece of that early, pioneering landscape.
