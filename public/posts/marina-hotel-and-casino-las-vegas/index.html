A Legacy of Retro Cool

Though the Marina Hotel and Casino is now just a memory, its legacy lives on. It represents a vital chapter in Las Vegas history, a bridge between the classic, intimate casinos of the past and the mega-resort era. The echoes of its lively casino, sophisticated dining, and vibrant entertainment can still be felt when reflecting on the evolution of the Las Vegas Strip. Today, the Marina’s footprint serves as a parking lot for the MGM Grand, but the stories of its heyday continue to intrigue those fascinated by vintage Las Vegas.
